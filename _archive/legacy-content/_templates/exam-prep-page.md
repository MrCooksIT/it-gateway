## Practice questions

### Lower order (recall)

1. Define [term].
2. List three [things].
3. What does [abbreviation] stand for?

### Middle order (application)

4. Explain how [process] works.
5. A user experiences [problem]. What is the most likely cause?

### Higher order (analysis/evaluation)

6. Compare [option A] and [option B] for a school that needs to [do something]. Recommend the better option and justify your answer.
7. Analyse the following scenario: [scenario]. Identify [problem] and suggest a solution.

## Marking tips

{: .highlight }
- Always read the scenario carefully — the context changes what counts as a correct answer
- "Name" = one word or short phrase (no explanation needed)
- "Explain" = give a reason or describe how/why
- "Discuss" = multiple points, often pros and cons
