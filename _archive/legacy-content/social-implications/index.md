# Social Implications

Social implications covers the impact of technology on society — from the digital divide and e-waste, through to cybercrime, privacy, and the ethical use of ICTs.

## What you will learn

| Grade | Focus |
|---|---|
| 10 | Software licensing, copyright, piracy, digital divide, economic impact of computers |
| 11 | Safety and security threats, social media impact, personal data |
| 12 | Cybercrime, national/international impact, distributed computing, information overload |

## Topics in this section

- [Social Implications Overview](./social-implications-overview) — ICT's impact on society, cybercrime, digital divide, responsible use (Gr 10–12)

## CAPS alignment

Social Implications appears in Paper 2 and is tested across all three grades. Questions are often scenario-based and require you to apply concepts to real-world situations.
