### ğŸ”„ Persistent

- Bullying can continue 24/7, even at home
- **No safe spaces** - the harassment follows victims everywhere

### ğŸ”’ Permanent  
- **Contributes to digital footprints** of both victim and bully

#### 1. Don't Respond to Harassment ğŸš«

#### 2. Don't Retaliate ğŸš«

#### 3. Don't Spread the Harassment ğŸš«

#### 4. Don't Believe the Bully ğŸš«

## ğŸ“š Key Takeaways
