Complications
Several legal and ethical concerns arise from the Internet
Access to copyrighted material
Anonymity
Censorship

Access to copyrighted material
Peer to peer networks allow people to share content
Often times shared content is not owned by the people sharing it
Easier than ever to distribute information or content that is not your own

Anonymity
Big question of how identifiable Internet users should be
Equality on the Internet
If all users are anonymous, users won’t be targeted or discriminated against
Cyberbullying
If all users are anonymous, no one is accountable for their actions

Censorship
Should Google display search results that have explicit or illegal content?
Should a government be able to filter what content its citizens see on the Internet?
