# Digital Technology (Grades 8–9)

This section covers the Digital Technology curriculum for Grades 8 and 9 — the foundation before the CAPS Information Technology course in Grades 10–12.

## Grade 8

| Topic | What it covers |
|-------|---------------|
| [What is Computing?](gr8/what-is-computing) | Introduction to computers, hardware vs software, how computers process information |
| [History of Computers](gr8/history) | Evolution from early machines to modern computing |
| [Karel Coding](gr8/karel-coding) | Introduction to programming logic using Karel in JavaScript |

## Grade 9

| Topic | What it covers |
|-------|---------------|
| [Digital Citizenship](gr9/digital-citizenship) | Responsible and safe use of technology, online conduct |
| [Web Design Basics](gr9/web-design) | Principles of good web design, layout, and user experience |
| [HTML & CSS](gr9/html-css) | Creating and styling web pages |
| [Internet & Browsers](gr9/internet-browsers) | How the internet works, browsers, URLs, and search |
