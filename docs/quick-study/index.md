# Quick Study

Quick Study pages are designed for **rapid revision** — especially useful the night before a test or exam. Each page is a condensed summary, cheat sheet or reference card for a key topic. They assume you have already studied the full content and just need a fast refresher.

## How to use Quick Study

1. **After a lesson** — skim the summary to confirm you understood the key points.
2. **Before a test** — read through the relevant summary page in 10–15 minutes.
3. **During exam prep** — use the cheat sheets as a self-test: cover the right column and try to recall each item.
